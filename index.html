About

CSS is the language we use to style and design web pages.

It helps us style our websites by adding text and background colors, fonts, layouts and so much more.

It is basically like the paint of the website.

The Box Model

The box model includes stylings like padding, border, margin and content.

Content - Text or Image.

Padding - Space inside the box.

Border - The line around the box.

Margin - Space outside the box.
